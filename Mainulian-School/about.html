About Mainulian School

Established

Mainulian School was established in 2008, dedicated to providing high-quality English education in Mirpur, Dhaka.

Our Vision

To be the leading educational institution in Bangladesh, fostering academic excellence and linguistic proficiency among students preparing for SSC and HSC examinations.

Our Mission

To empower students with strong English language skills through personalized coaching, small batch sizes, and a disciplined learning environment that builds confidence and prepares them for future success.

Why Mainulian School?

  • Personalized coaching approach tailored to each student's needs
  • Experienced and dedicated instructors with proven track records
  • Small batch sizes ensuring individual attention
  • Strong focus on English language proficiency
  • 99.9% success rate for SSC and HSC examinations
  • Disciplined learning environment that builds confidence
  • Over 10,000 students successfully taught
  • Holistic student development beyond academics
